Golden Lion
This deliverable has evolved from an outdated email with PDF attachments of results, to an updated email with PDF attachments, to finally a memo with a link to a results landing page.

Every month we would have to plug in the numbers that were previously in the PDF attachments into the the html. Which wasn't too bad, until we were requested to also rearrange the order of each division, depending on the results. This turned into a 3+ hours ordeal, with tons of room for error.

I took it upon myself to implement a JQuery library and custom javascript to dynamically build out, and pull in the results from the excel documents the account team provided, turning our 3 hour headache into a 5 minute breeze.

Did I mention it's also mobile responsive?
