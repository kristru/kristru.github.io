Golden Lion

  • Category: Design, Developement
  • Skill: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript(D3.js)
  • Deliverables: High fidelity wireframe and web page build

The Golden Lion is performance stats across Richmond Americans various divisions. These initial emails provided links to a PDF for each category. My first objective when hired was to create a more appealing and modern design to increase engagement with stakeholders. Depsite the cleaner and more engaging visuals, the workflow efficiency and time to create the PDFs were still an issue. The print team had to go through rounds of revisions with edits and reviews, update and provide files, which the PM's would have to provide to us, which we would have to resave and review again, etc. It just wasn't practical.

Our solution was to create a landing page that the numbers could be plugged into and could be quickly updated if needed, removing the need for print design, files and multiple reviews. After a short period of time the powers that be, wanted the results to be posted based on leaders and numbers. Which meant This created more time consuming inefficiencies.

Between projects and on light workdays, I took it upon myself to integrate D3.js, a JavaScript library that helps bring data to life with DOM manipulation, into the landing page. In doing this, I took a 3+ hour task to a 5 minute task.
